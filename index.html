Rock climbing, in the modern sense, is the art of scaling steep ascents using all kinds and sizes of hand and foot holds. It is practiced on a wall with many features and varying angles. The efforts of climbers have transformed sports climbing into a growing, global sport (around 75 countries participate in the world cup), getting widespread attention after inclusion in Tokyo Olympics 2020. There are good reasons to climb too, it has been consistently rated as one of the top healthiest sports. It is a one stop shop for physical and mental strength, flexibility, cardio and stress reduction. The climbing wall at IITK, first among the educational institutes of India, aims to provide INCLUSIVE recreational opportunities and environment that foster group activity and an appreciation of diversity. We put safety as our first priority, and strongly believe that proper climbing is safe climbing. Our coach, Mr. Pushpender Singh, is a national level climber and provides training to newcomers in climbing skills as well as safety procedures. The climbing wall is in New SAC (impossible to miss). It has 3 sections of varying texture and slopes (height 11.4 m). The first two sections are of 2 m and the last section is of 2.85 m.
